- Launch CrypTool application
- Select **RSA Demonstration** option

`text`

`Prime number p: 211
Prime number q: 233`

phi(N) = (p-1)(q-1): 48720

`textA#b#h#i#s#h#e#k #J#a#m#d#a#d#e
Numbers: 065 #098 #104 #105 #115 #104 #101 #107 #074 #097 #109 #100 #097 #100 #101`

## **Step 4: Generate Ciphertext**

**Encryption formula:** **`c[i] = m[i]^e (mod N)`**

**Result:**

`text`

`Ciphertext: 03316 #24394 #23366 #20714 #06205 #23366 #07428 #30861 #46430 #18504 #33521 #42283`

## **Step 5: Decrypt Back to Plaintext**

**Decryption formula:** **`m[i] = c[i]^d (mod N)`**

**Result:**

`text`

`Decrypted: AbhishekJamdade`
